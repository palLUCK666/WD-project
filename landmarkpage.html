Iconic Monuments

  • Taj Mahal: One of the Seven Wonders of the World, located in Agra, symbolizing eternal love.
  • Red Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, showcasing Mughal architecture and history.
  • Hawa Mahal: The "Palace of Winds" in Jaipur, famous for its intricate latticework and royal history.
  • Charminar: A famous mosque in Hyderabad, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture and an important historical site.
