North Indian Cuisine

  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, tomato-based curry.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spiced potato.
  • Idli and Sambar: Soft rice cakes with lentil stew.
  • Chettinad Chicken: A fiery, flavorful dish from Tamil Nadu.

South Indian favorite: Masala Dosa

Sweet Delights

  • Gulab Jamun: Fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft cheese balls in light syrup.
  • Payasam: A sweet milk-rice dessert from the South.
